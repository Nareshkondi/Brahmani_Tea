Our Heritage

From The Hills Of
Assam To Your Cup

Brahmani Tea is rooted in the rich heritage of Assam, where lush green plantations produce some of the world's finest tea leaves. Nestled along the Brahmaputra river valley, our gardens thrive in soil enriched by centuries of alluvial deposits.

Every blend captures the bold flavour, rich aroma, and comforting warmth that defines authentic Indian chai — connecting you to generations of tea craftsmanship.
